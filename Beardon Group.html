The Beardon Group, LLC

Welcome to the Beardon Group

Based in Stillwater, OK, The Beardon Group is a boutique computer services consulting company. From sophisticated, SCORM compliant learning management systems to state of the art hosting facilities and everything in between, The Beardon Group can offer you the resources and expertise to make your project idea a reality. We specialize in website design, software application engineering, information systems integration, and e-Learning solutions. With a team of full-time senior developers and an assortment of specialized part-time staff, the Beardon Group is confident that we can give your project the attention it deserves.

We pride ourselves in developing long-term relationships with our clients. We have been providing computer technology services to Oklahoma State University for more than 20 years. We know our customers have a choice of service partners. We strive to be a technology provider that functions at the highest level of integrity and provides dynamic and effective solutions designed to fit our customers' individual needs.

Our Pledge

The professionals of the Beardon Group guarantee your satisfaction. No project is considered complete until all parties are fully satisfied.
