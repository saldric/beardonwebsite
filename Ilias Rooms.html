Ilias Rooms, Your E-Learning Solution

Welcome to Ilias Rooms: the best choice for your e-learning needs.

We provide top of the line hosting services for Ilias, the first open source learning management software to reach SCORM 2004 (3rd Edition) compliance. Additionally, we offer a full range of support options to meet your specific needs. A wide variety of enhancements are available to customize your Ilias experience. From idea conception to roll out, we will guide you every step of the way to ensure that you have the e-learning solution that perfectly meets your needs.

Our software-as-a-service model provides clients with an attention to detail unmatched by other service providers. Our professionals are here to serve you, and we will cut through the complexity of e-learning courses. Furthermore, once your personalized e-learning system is fully converted and operational, take comfort knowing that your system is stored and protected in a state-of-the-art facility. Our hosting network and datacenter is housed in a Tier IV datacenter facility inside the INFOMART telecom hotel near downtown Dallas. By hosting “in the cloud”, we can guarantee reliability, scalability, and security.
