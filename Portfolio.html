Fire Protection Publications

For over fifteen years now, Beardon has been the network architect and manager for FPP, the international leader in the creation and publication of fire training materials. Beardon implemented and currently manages FPP servers in a multiple platform environment, including an AS400, Macintosh, Windows, and VMWare machines. Beardon has also worked on three separate Hazardous Material web-based courses for FPP. Each course is composed of more than one thousand pages of multi-media content, including Adobe Flash, audio, video and interactive, branching and non-sequential text. The development of this E-learning system was part of a cooperative project between the U.S. Air Force and FPP. Beardon deconstructed the original course then rebuilt it from the original raw files while the coding in the system was re-written to meet the requirements of a SCORM compliant learning management system.

Oklahoma Fire Service Training

Beardon created the core application for OFST, a state agency, giving training agents across the state of Oklahoma remote access to an integrated database which tracks income, expenses, equipment check-in/check-out, employee time/location and other important data. This application also allows the training agents to implement, manage, approve, and monitor courses as well as download rosters and track those progressing through the training material.

American Coupon Company

Beardon developed software which enabled ACC to provide loan payment coupon books for over five thousand financial institutions. The appplication handled printing, invoicing, and remote data submissions, among its many other functions.

OSU Physical Plant Services

Beardon Services created the core application for a work group of over seven hundred employees integrating personnel data, purchasing and inventory as well as maintenance scheduling for over seven million square feet of built space spread across the campus of Oklahoma State University
