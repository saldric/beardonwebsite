Web Hosting

Beardon Services hosting network is housed in a Tier IV datacenter facility inside the INFOMART telecom hotel near downtown Dallas. The Beardon network lies on three redundant electrical grids; is powered by TXU; and includes five, one hundred ton, on site water chillers that deliver N+1 cooling to support the HVAC needs. It is no surprise that businesses such as MCI, Level 3, Equinix, Veriq, Switch & Data, Verizon, and DataSides are also serviced along with Beardon Services in the telecom hotel.

Whether you need our hosting services for your website or your e-learning system, you can rest assured that the Beardon Services will provide you with the best possible service from this state-of-the art facility.

Network Administration

If your business or organization requires a network administrator, our network specialists can develop the network design and ensure that your network has the proper security. They will troubleshoot and debug any network related problems. Their work will also include the maintenance of the network's authorization infrastructure as well as network backup systems.

Our professionals will advise your organization on new hardware purchases, assist in rolling out new software installations, maintain disk images for new computer installations, maintain standards for server installations and applications, and monitor the performance of the network.

The professionals at Beardon Services have both the breadth of technical knowledge and the ability to learn the ins and outs of new networking and server software packages quickly, thus keeping your network running smoothly.
