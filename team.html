Meet The Team

Creative, Diverse, and Ready to Solve Any Problem

Jim Wirt, Founder and CEO of Beardon Services

"I began as an independent software programmer in 1985 as a student pursuing a Computer Science degree at OSU. After I graduated I continued doing computer projects for various departments related to OSU and other businesses (Kicker). In 1986 the business changed it's name to Beardon Services Inc. and provided network maintenance and software development services. The "why" question is because I think building the business is what God wants me to do for a career. I see the company as being an oportunity to provide a job opportunity and financial opportunity for people that God connects me with. I also see it as a revenue source to use to spread the gospel. I think young people can be helped by seeing a business model God's principles of integrity and service to others.

I provide computer network support for our customers. I provide xTuple customization and implementation for our xTuple customers. I develop software as needed for our assigned projects. I provide owner level management regarding projects and business strategy."

Hobbies and interests outside of work - "I enjoy playing softball on a "senior" league. I am a pastor and enjoy spending time with the people I'm in relationship with. I enjoy reaching out to needy and hurting people both locally and overseas. I enjoy researching stuff and learning things from the internet. I enjoy OSU sports and spending quality time with my wife."

Aaron Bean, Director of Development

In 2005, Aaron Bean, previously with Lucent Technologies, began heading the development team. Aaron quickly made a name for himself during a series of successful projects; including the design of a client database application for OSU Fire Service Training that runs their day-today operation, a wevsite allowing for student registration, and an E-learning solution platform for IFSTA/FPP
